# Zia Fashion Point - Business Discovery Program

Assalamu Alaikum Bhai,

Main Zia Fashion Point ke liye ek Smart Shop Management Software banana chahta hu.

Is software ka maksad Tally ko replace karna nahi hai.

Balki shop ke daily kaam ko aur easy banana hai.

---

## Is Software se Aapko Kya Fayde Honge?

Ye software aapke business ko next level par le jayega. Kuch bade fayde ye hain:

*   **Aapko Aazadi (Freedom):** Aap duniya me kahin bhi hon, mobile se dekh sakte hain ki shop me kya ho raha hai, kitni sale hui, aur kitna stock hai.

*   **Time ki Bachat:** Jo kaam register me ghanto lagte hain, wo software me minto me ho jayega.

*   **Hisaab-Kitaab me Aasani:** Roz ka hisaab, stock ki ginti, aur kaun sa maal zyada bik raha hai, sab ek click par pata chal jayega.

*   **Chori aur Hera-Pheri se Bachaav:** Har cheez system me record hogi. Isse cash ya stock me koi gadbad nahi kar payega.

*   **Stock par Poora Control:** Aapko hamesha pata rahega ki kaun sa item, size, ya color stock me hai. Isse 'maal nahi hai' kehne ki naubat nahi aayegi aur dead stock bhi kam hoga.

*   **Grahak se Behtar Rishta:** Aap customer ka mobile number aur unki pichli kharidari save kar sakte hain. Isse aap unhe offers bhej sakte hain aur unki pasand ka maal dikha sakte hain.

*   **Business Badaane me Madad (Growth):** Aapko pata chalega ki customer kya pasand kar rahe hain. Is data se aap business ke liye behtar decision le sakenge.

Isliye main pehle aapke business ko samajhna chahta hu.
