# **Module 14: Financial Planning Framework – A to Z**

## **Net Worth क्या है?**

Net Worth = **Total Assets** – **Total Liabilities**

* **Assets** = वो चीजें जो तुम्हारी ownership में हैं और जिनकी कोई monetary value है (cash, property, investments, gold, आदि)
* **Liabilities** = वो सारे loans, debts, या financial obligations जो तुम्हें चुकाने हैं

**Example:**
अगर तुम्हारे पास:

* Bank balance: ₹2,00,000
* Mutual Funds: ₹5,00,000
* Gold: ₹1,00,000
* Home value: ₹40,00,000
  **Total Assets** = ₹48,00,000

और तुम्हारे ऊपर:

* Home Loan: ₹25,00,000
* Car Loan: ₹3,00,000
  **Total Liabilities** = ₹28,00,000

तो:
**Net Worth** = ₹48,00,000 – ₹28,00,000 = **₹20,00,000**

---

## **Net Worth Calculation के Steps**

1. **सभी Assets की List बनाओ**

   * Cash & Bank balance
   * Investments (Mutual Funds, Stocks, Bonds, PPF, FD, etc.)
   * Real Estate (market value)
   * Precious metals (Gold, Silver, etc.)
   * Retirement funds (EPF, NPS, etc.)

2. **सभी Liabilities की List बनाओ**

   * Home Loan, Car Loan, Personal Loan
   * Credit Card dues
   * Education Loan
   * किसी भी प्रकार का unpaid debt

3. **Formula Apply करो**

   ```
   Net Worth = (Total Assets) – (Total Liabilities)
   ```

4. **Track करो हर साल/छः महीने में**

   * Year-on-year comparison से पता चलेगा wealth बढ़ रही है या घट रही है

## **Net Worth बढ़ाने के Tips**

* Liabilities जल्दी खत्म करो
* Assets को diversify करो (Equity, Debt, Gold, Real Estate)
* Passive income sources बनाओ
* हर साल Net Worth growth target रखो (e.g., +15% per year)
